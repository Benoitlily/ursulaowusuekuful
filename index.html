I, Honorable Ursula Owusu-Ekuful, assumed office as the first Member of Parliament for Ablekuma West Constituency, with a goal to work for and with my constituents, to realize the hopes and aspirations of my people. Over my 2 tenures as Member of Parliament, I have made best use of the resources at my disposal to create a participatory, accountable and responsive Ablekuma West for all.
